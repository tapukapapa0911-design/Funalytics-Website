FAQ

Everything you need to know about Funalytics, the scoring model, and the tools.

Funalytics is a free mutual fund analytics platform that scores and ranks 600+ SEBI-registered funds across 19 categories using a research-backed model covering returns, risk-adjusted performance, and valuation. It also includes an Investment Planner and a Fund Comparison tool.

Yes, completely free. No login required, no subscription, no paywall. Open the app and start exploring immediately.

Each fund is evaluated within its SEBI category and weighted to produce a final score out of 100. Higher scores indicate stronger risk-adjusted returns combined with better valuation metrics.

The Investment Planner takes your monthly SIP or lumpsum amount, time horizon, and risk appetite, then outputs a category-wise portfolio allocation — across Large Cap, Mid Cap, ELSS, Hybrid, Debt, and more — tailored to your goals.

Funalytics was built by Ameen Khan, an PGPM Finance student at IBS Mumbai, during a Summer Internship Programme at Vivek Damani Financial Services LLP a SEBI-registered mutual fund distributor. It was designed to solve real fund-selection friction for retail investors.
